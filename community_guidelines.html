Community Guidelines

Last updated: 22.07.23

1. Introduction:

Welcome to the TalisMarket community! Our community guidelines are designed to create a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all users. By participating in our community, you agree to abide by these guidelines.

2. Respectful Behavior:

Be respectful and considerate towards others. Avoid offensive, harmful, or discriminatory language and behavior. Treat fellow community members with kindness and understanding.

3. No Hate Speech or Harassment:

Hate speech, harassment, or any form of intimidation towards individuals or groups based on their race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or other personal characteristics will not be tolerated.

4. No Spam or Self-Promotion:

Do not engage in spamming, excessive self-promotion, or any form of solicitation in our community. Respect the purpose of discussions and interactions.

5. Stay On Topic:

Keep your contributions relevant to the community's purpose. Off-topic discussions may be subject to removal.

6. Respect Intellectual Property:

Do not post or share copyrighted material without the necessary rights or permissions. Respect the intellectual property of others and give proper credit when necessary.

7. Report Inappropriate Content:

If you come across any content that violates these guidelines or is otherwise inappropriate, please report it to the moderators or administrators immediately.

8. Enforcement:

Failure to comply with these community guidelines may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from the community. Decisions regarding enforcement will be made at the discretion of the moderators or administrators.

9. Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about our Community Guidelines or need to report any violations, please contact us through our website TalisMarket.

We encourage all community members to contribute positively and foster a supportive environment for everyone. Let's build a great community together!

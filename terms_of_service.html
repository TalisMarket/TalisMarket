2.2 Communication: We may use your email address to send you important updates, newsletters, promotional offers, and other relevant information about our services. You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the email.

2.5 Aggregated Data: We may use aggregated and anonymized data for statistical and analytical purposes to improve our services, but such data will not identify any individual.

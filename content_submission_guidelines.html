Content Submission Guidelines

Last updated: 22.07.23

1. Introduction:

Thank you for your interest in submitting content to TalisMarket! We value the creativity and contributions of our users. Before submitting any content, please review the following guidelines:

2. Original and Non-Infringing Content:

By submitting content, you affirm that you are the original creator of the content, or you have the necessary rights, licenses, and permissions to submit the content. Do not submit content that infringes on the intellectual property rights of others, including copyrights, trademarks, or any proprietary information.

3. Appropriate Content:

Content submissions must adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Must not be offensive, harmful, or inappropriate, including content that promotes violence, discrimination, or illegal activities.
  • Must not contain any personal or sensitive information of individuals without their explicit consent.
  • Must not contain viruses, malware, or any harmful code.
  • Must not violate any applicable laws or regulations.
  • Must not be spam or excessive promotional content.

5. Content Review:

TalisMarket reserves the right to review, modify, or reject any content submissions at our sole discretion. We may remove any content that violates our guidelines or for any other reason deemed appropriate.

6. Copyright Complaints:

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used on our website in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please contact us immediately with the necessary information for prompt resolution.
